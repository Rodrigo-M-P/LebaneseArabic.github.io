Article, Demonstratives, Idāfe

  • Article l-: assimilates before sun letters (e.g., l shams → sh‑shams); normal before moon letters (l 2amar).
  • Demonstratives: near → hayda/hayde/hol; far → haydak/haydik/hawlik/holik.
  • Idāfe: N1 N2 (“of”); or taba3 + clitic for clarity.

Prepositions + Clitics

  • ma3 (with): ma3e, ma3ak, ma3ik, ma3o, ma3a, ma3na, ma3kon, ma3on
  • 3ala (on/to): 3alayye, 3alayk, 3alayki, 3al…
  • bi (in/with): b‑ (bound form)
  • la (to/for): layye, layk…
  • min (from): menni, mennak… (min + l → mnel/men l)
  • 3and (at/with): 3ande, 3andak…
Drop b‑ after badd‑/lazem/ra7/7a/la7/la/2abl ma/ba3d ma. Imperatives use the base.

Counting & Pluralization Rules

  • Dual (spoken): add -en (masc) / -ten (fem): yawmen (2 days), kalben (2 dogs), jome3ten (2 weeks), d2i2ten (2 minutes).
  • 3–10: number + plural noun: tlet kleb (3 dogs), tlet tiyyem (3 days).
  • 11–19: number + singular: 7da3shar se3a (11 hours).
  • 20+: number + singular: 3eshrin se3a (20 hours).

Time-Telling Expressions

  • X:15 = X w robe3/rebe3 — 12:15 → tna3esh w robe3
  • X:30 = X w nos — 12:30 → tna3esh w nos
  • X:45 = (X+1) ella robe3/rebe3 — 12:45 → wehde ella robe3
  • X:50 = (X+1) ella 3ashra — 12:50 → wehde ella 3ashra
