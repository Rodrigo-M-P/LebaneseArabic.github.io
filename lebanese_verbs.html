Patterns & Notes

  • Present/Habitual = b- + base. Progressive: 3am + base. Future: ra7/7a/la7 + base. Imperative uses base.
  • Drop b- after badd-, lazem, moumken, la, 2abl ma, ba3d ma, ra7/7a/la7.
  • Negation: ma + (b-present), ma 3am + base, ma + past, mish (nominal), ma fi (existential).
